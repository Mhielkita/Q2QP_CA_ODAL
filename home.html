ABOUT US
Background
The Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting animal welfare in the Philippines. Known for its animal rescue and rehabilitation efforts, PAWS provides shelter, medical care, and advocacy for animals facing abuse or abandonment. The organization actively engages in educational programs, community outreach, and legislative initiatives to raise awareness about responsible pet ownership and improve animal welfare laws in the country. PAWS also operates an animal shelter, facilitates adoption programs, and plays a vital role in emergency response efforts during natural disasters.
What We've Done
Some of their key activities include rescuing and rehabilitating animals in distress, advocating for improved animal welfare legislation in the Philippines, conducting educational programs to raise awareness about responsible pet ownership, engaging in community outreach to address local animal welfare concerns, operating an animal shelter for rescued animals, facilitating adoption programs, and playing a crucial role in emergency response during natural disasters. PAWS has been instrumental in addressing the needs of animals facing abuse, abandonment, or emergencies, working towards creating a more humane environment for animals in the country.
